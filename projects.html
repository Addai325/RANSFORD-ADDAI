Overview

As part of a collaborative bootcamp project, I played a key role in developing the frontend of a website dedicated to improving the well-being of seniors in our community.

Description

Our team, comprised of passionate healthcare professionals, shared a common mission to enhance the lives of seniors. I took the lead in crafting the user interface and design elements of the website using HTML and CSS. My focus was on creating an intuitive and visually appealing frontend that aligns with our vision of cultivating an engaged community of seniors.
